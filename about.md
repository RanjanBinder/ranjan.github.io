I’m **Ranjan**, a Business Intelligence Analyst who enjoys the space between
raw data and business decisions. My day-to-day involves writing SQL, building
dashboards, translating stakeholder questions into analyses, and documenting
requirements so engineers, analysts, and business teams stay aligned.

## How I work

- **Understand the question first.** Before any query, I make sure I know what
  decision the analysis will drive.
- **Start simple, then layer.** I build queries clause by clause, sanity-check
  intermediate results, and only then optimize.
- **Write it down.** Whether it’s a BRD, a metric definition, or a runbook,
  documentation is part of the deliverable — not an afterthought.

## Currently

I’m deepening my expertise in advanced SQL (window functions, query planning,
temporal joins), expanding into data science and analytics engineering, and
working through a structured learning path benchmarked against MIT-level rigor.

## Beyond work

I keep a running interview-prep curriculum in Notion, practice SQL daily on the
MySQL `employees` database, and apply a 5-step framework I’ve refined for
